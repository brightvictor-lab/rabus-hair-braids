Our Story

More Than Braids.
It's An Art Form.

Rabus Hair Africa Braiding is a professional African hair braiding studio based in Chicago, Illinois, known for delivering high-quality, stylish, and long-lasting braids. Our skilled stylists specialize in a variety of popular styles, including Knotless Braids, Boho Knotless Braids, Box Braids, Feed-In Braids, Two-Strand Twists, and Crochet Installations, all tailored to suit your unique look.

We are committed to providing a clean, calm, and premium experience using top-quality braiding hair to ensure beautiful results while protecting your natural hair. Appointments are required to ensure each client receives our full attention and top-tier service.
